Game Controls

Player 1

  • Move Left: A
  • Move Right: D
  • Jump: W
  • Block: B
  • Punch: J
  • Kick: K
  • Shoot Projectile: P
  • Ultimate: U

Player 2 (or Enemy in 2-player mode)

  • Move Left: Arrow Left
  • Move Right: Arrow Right
  • Jump: Arrow Up
  • Block: /
  • Punch: . (dot)
  • Kick: , (comma)
  • Shoot Projectile: Shift
  • Ultimate: Enter
